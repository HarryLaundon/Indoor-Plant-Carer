Welcome to Indoor Plant Carer

This webpage will help you to look after your green friends on a weekly basis

Below are 3 house plants

Click the "Not Watered!" button for the plant you have just watered with a 500ml can or jug. Each click will register this, but rememeber: some plants need more water than others so make sure to click until the button says it's had enough. Otherwise you might overwater it and stunt its growth!

If it helps, remember to water the Monstera 3 times, the Cactus once and the Calatheas twice!

Watering Guide

Plants

  • Monstera Deliciosa
  • Cactus
  • Calatheas

If you overwatered any of the plants, don't worry!

Just move the plant to somewhere with some direct light if possible, it will help the unessecary water be absorbed faster and our green friend will be back to normal in no time.
